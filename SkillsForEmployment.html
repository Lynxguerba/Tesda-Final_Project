Programs & Services

We provides a wide range of programs and services aimed at enhancing the skills and knowledge of Filipinos. Its offerings include skills training that equips individuals with the necessary competencies to perform specific jobs or tasks, assessment and certification services that validate the acquired competencies of individuals, scholarship programs that support learners who cannot afford the cost of education, and industry partnerships that help bridge the gap between industry needs and workforce development. These programs and services are designed to help individuals achieve their full potential and contribute to the economic growth of the country.

Skills For Employment

Whether you're a fresh graduate looking for your first job or an experienced professional seeking to enhance your skills, TESDA has something for you. Here are some of the top skills for employment that you can acquire through TESDA:

Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Skills

In today's digital age, ICT skills are in high demand across industries. TESDA offers various ICT courses, such as Computer Systems Servicing, Programming, and Web Development, to name a few. These courses provide hands-on training on hardware and software systems, as well as programming languages and web development tools. Upon completion, you can pursue a career in IT support, software development, or web design and development, among others.

Health Care Skills

With the growing need for health care services, TESDA offers courses in health care skills, such as Caregiving, Dental Technology, and Medical Transcription. These courses provide theoretical and practical training on various aspects of health care, from patient care to medical transcription. Upon completion, you can pursue a career as a caregiver, dental technician, or medical transcriptionist, among others.

Hospitality and Tourism Skills

The hospitality and tourism industry is a major contributor to the Philippine economy, and TESDA offers courses in this field to help individuals pursue careers in this industry. Courses such as Cookery, Food and Beverage Services, and Housekeeping provide hands-on training on various aspects of hospitality and tourism, from food preparation to guest services. Upon completion, you can pursue a career as a cook, waiter/waitress, or hotel housekeeper, among others.

Construction Skills

Construction is a vital industry that contributes to the growth and development of the country. TESDA offers various courses in construction skills, such as Carpentry, Plumbing, and Masonry, to name a few. These courses provide hands-on training on various aspects of construction, from blueprint reading to actual construction work. Upon completion, you can pursue a career as a carpenter, plumber, or mason, among others.

Automotive Skills

With the growing number of vehicles on the road, there is a high demand for skilled automotive technicians. TESDA offers courses in Automotive Servicing that provide hands-on training on various aspects of automotive repair and maintenance. Upon completion, you can pursue a career as an automotive technician, mechanic, or service advisor, among others.

Summmary

We offers a variety of courses and programs aimed at providing Filipinos with the technical and vocational education and training necessary to secure employment and pursue career opportunities. TESDA offers courses in Information and Communications Technology (ICT) skills, health care skills, hospitality and tourism skills, construction skills, and automotive skills. These courses provide hands-on training on various aspects of each industry, allowing individuals to develop the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their chosen field. By enrolling in a TESDA course, individuals can take the first step towards a brighter future.
